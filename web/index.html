Austin

An Educational Chatbot by Julian Weng

Find Out More

Austin is a chatbot which allows users to practice applying numerical relationships learned in class in a conversational format.

Students can reinforce learning at any time with questions customized with their answer history.

Questions

Flexibility

Handles different ways of speech and asking for problems.

Breadth

Handles any kind of discipline modelled with numerical relationships, just add to a .csv file!

Customization

Adapts question topics and variables based on past progress.
